Frequently Asked Questions

What is this platform for?

This platform is designed for composers and audio editors to collaborate on creating and generating music samples. Composers can generate music based on selected instruments, genres, and settings, while audio editors assist in refining and implementing the generated samples.

Who can use this platform?

The platform is intended for composers and audio editors. Composers can generate and download music samples and audio editors can review and edit generated samples.

What roles are available on the platform?

There are two main roles: Composer (for generating and downloading music samples) and Audio Editor (for reviewing and editing generated samples). Admins manage the system and content.

Will I get a copyright claim for using music from AIMGNN?

Ideally, you should not get a copyright claim for using music created with AIMGNN.

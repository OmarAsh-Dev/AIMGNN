Generation Parameters

What is the maximum duration (length) of a generated piece?

The maximum length of a generated piece is 2 minutes.

What is the minimum duration (length) of a generated piece?

The minimum duration of a generated piece is 10 seconds.

What is Temperature in the generation parameters?

Temperature is a parameter that controls the randomness of the generated music. A higher temperature will produce more random music, while a lower temperature will produce more predictable music.

What is Top P in the generation parameters?

Top P is a parameter that controls the diversity of the generated music. A higher top P will produce more diverse music, while a lower top P will produce more similar music.

What is Top K in the generation parameters?

Top K is a parameter that controls the diversity of the generated music. A higher top K will produce more diverse music, while a lower top K will produce more similar music.

What is cfg_coef in the generation parameters?

cfg_coef is a parameter that controls the consistency of the generated music. A higher cfg_coef will produce more consistent music, while a lower cfg_coef will produce more random music.

What is two_step_cfg in the generation parameters?

two_step_cfg is a parameter that controls the consistency of the generated music. A higher two_step_cfg will produce more consistent music, while a lower two_step_cfg will produce more random music.

What is extend_stride in the generation parameters?

extend_stride is a parameter that affects melody continuity over extended durations
